INTRODUCTION

EVERY KEYSTROKE
SPAWNS A LIFE FORM.

You are the creator. The characters you type become living organisms — each one genetically encoded by its letter, each one fighting to survive in the ecosystem you build. This guide tells you how to read their world.

// HOW TO PLAY

any letter / number Spawns one creature. Its DNA is determined by the character and its position in your sequence. BACKSPACE Kills the most recently spawned creature with a dissolution animation. hover cursor Identifies the nearest creature — shows species, family, age, speed, and eye count.

On mobile, tap anywhere on the screen to bring up the keyboard. The canvas automatically resizes so your creatures stay visible.

// THE FIVE FAMILIES

Every printable character belongs to exactly one family. The family shapes the creature's size, speed, temperament, and role in the food chain.

// THE FOOD CHAIN

Predators actively chase prey within their perception radius (~120 px). Prey flee in inverse proportion to distance — the closer the predator, the more frantic the escape.

A predator that catches prey gains +0.42 energy. The prey dissolves in a burst of particles. Its energy feeds the hunter.

// ENERGY & SURVIVAL

Every creature has an energy reserve. It drains continuously at a slow rate. When energy hits zero, the creature starves — same death animation as a manual delete. Energy drain only activates when 5 or more creatures are alive, so small experiments never die on their own.

The ring that appears around a creature below 55% energy is your early warning system. A pulsing red ring means the creature will die within seconds unless it finds food. An apex predator kept well-fed can outlast many prey populations.

// REPRODUCTION

The ecosystem can grow beyond what you type. When two creatures of the same family stay within 50 px of each other and both have energy above 72%, they reproduce.

The offspring inherits a blend of both parents' DNA — color, size, speed, and body shape are mixed, then nudged by up to ±12% mutation. Both parents spend 0.28 energy and enter a 6-second cooldown.

Population is capped at 80 living creatures. If you type a long sentence and leave it running, the ecosystem will self-regulate — predators thin the herds, survivors reproduce, and a dynamic equilibrium emerges.
